In September 2020, I left The Times to start Ambrook with two long-time friends, Mackenzie and Jeff. At Ambrook, we help farmers and ranchers find farm funding. Our first products include a searchable farm funding library with over 200 grants, loans, and other financial assistance. We also help automate the application process, removing the hassle of dealing with complex paperwork and processes.
